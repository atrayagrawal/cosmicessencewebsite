The Founder

Atray's Story

I didn't set out to be an educator. I set out to understand how things work — and more importantly, why some things work better than others.

That curiosity led me through five ventures, countless failures, and eventually to a realization: the mental models that helped me navigate uncertainty weren't taught in school. They were hidden in physics textbooks, in the patterns of history, in the stories of founders who built before me.

Cosmic Essence started as a simple question: What if we taught teenagers to see the world like founders do — using physics as the lens?

Not physics as a subject to memorize. Physics as a way to understand momentum, leverage, critical mass, and activation energy. The same concepts that power rockets also power startups.

Today, we've taught 159+ students, watched them get into top universities, build real ventures, and most importantly — develop a way of seeing that will serve them for life.

The Long View

A 100-Year Institution

This isn't a tutoring company. This isn't a coaching business. Cosmic Essence is being built to last a century.

We're creating a new model of education — one that combines rigorous thinking with real-world building. One that treats teenagers as capable of more than compliance. One that produces alumni who go on to solve important problems, not just accumulate credentials.

The programs we run today are just the beginning. The book, the community, the methodology — these are the foundations of something much larger.

Parallel Work

Kythera

Before Cosmic Essence, there was Kythera — a community I built for founders and builders. 925+ members. Dozens of events. Real relationships.

Kythera taught me what community really means. Not numbers. Not engagement metrics. Trust. The kind where someone will take your call at 2 AM because they know you'll do the same for them.

Cosmic Essence brings that same ethos to education. Small cohorts. Real relationships. Long-term thinking.
