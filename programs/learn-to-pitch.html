Program Overview

The Art of Expression

A 10-day confidence and expression intensive, co-facilitated with Vidhi (ABT). This isn't just about pitching ideas — it's about finding your voice and learning to stand in your power.

Three-Phase Arc

Days 1–3

Presence & Psychological Safety

Led by Vidhi. Building the foundation of confidence from the inside out.

Days 4–7

Idea Development

Led by Atray. Structuring your thinking, building your narrative.

Days 8–10

Delivery & Final Pitch

Co-facilitated. Putting it all together. The pitch as proof of transformation.

Who This Is For

Grades 6+ · Mixed-Age Cohorts

This program welcomes students from grade 6 onwards. The mixed-age format creates a unique learning environment where younger students learn from older ones — and everyone discovers their voice.
